Witold Pilecki

Voluntary prisoner of the KL Auschwitz

Officer of the Polish Army reserves, cofounder of the Polish Secret Army, voluntary prisoner of the KL Auschwitz, officer of the Main Headquarters of the Home Army and 'NIE', political prisoner of the Stalinist period, the victim of a judiciary murder.

TIMELINE

  • 1901 - he was born in the area of Nowogródczyzna, Poland.
  • 1910 - his mother Ludwika and his brothers and sisters, moves to Vilnus. He starts his education there in elementary school of commerce.
  • 1914 - I World War begins. Mother takes children to her mother's in Hawryłków, Mohylewszczyzna. The kids went to a new school in Orle – a small Borderland (Kresy) town, where Witold set up the first boy scout regiment and begun organizing self-education courses.
